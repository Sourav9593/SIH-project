  • Teracotta Art

    Terracotta art in India has a long and storied history, dating back to the Indus Valley Civilization and continuing through various periods in Indian history. Terracotta, a type of earthenware, is made from natural clay that is shaped and then fired at a low temperature to harden. This medium was widely used due to the availability of clay and the relative ease of working with it.
